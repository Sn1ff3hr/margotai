Privacy Policy

Marxia Cafe y Bocaditos (“we”, “us”) is committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the data gathered when you browse our menu, place an order, or interact with the Marxia Cafe y Bocaditos web experience.

1. Data We Collect

  • Order details: items selected, delivery preferences, and special instructions.
  • Contact information: name, phone number, email address, billing and delivery address, and Ecuadorian ID/RUC when required for invoicing.
  • Technical telemetry: browser type, device characteristics, and anonymized analytics when consent is granted.
  • Location: optional GPS coordinates supplied to improve delivery accuracy.

2. How We Use Data

  • To confirm and fulfill orders, coordinate delivery, and provide customer support.
  • To comply with fiscal and regulatory obligations, including invoicing and food safety recordkeeping.
  • To improve our products and services through aggregated, consent-based analytics.
  • To secure our systems, monitor for fraud, and maintain audit trails aligned with PCI DSS Requirement 10.

3. Legal Bases

We process personal data on the basis of contract performance (fulfilling your order), legitimate interest (service optimization and fraud prevention), and consent (marketing or analytics where required). You may withdraw consent at any time through the cookie banner or by contacting us directly.

4. Sharing & Retention

  • We share data with trusted processors such as payment gateways or delivery partners strictly to complete your order.
  • Vendors must meet NIST CSF, CISA Cyber Essentials, and PCI DSS safeguards under written agreements.
  • Order records are retained for up to five years to satisfy accounting and regulatory requirements, after which they are securely deleted or anonymized.

5. Security Measures

  • TLS encryption for data in transit (PCI DSS Req. 4) and hardened storage for necessary records (PCI DSS Req. 3).
  • Role-based access control, multi-factor authentication, and least-privilege provisioning (NIST PR.AC, PCI DSS Req. 7-8).
  • Continuous monitoring, vulnerability management, and incident response plans in line with NIST Detect/Respond and CISA guidance.

6. Your Rights

You may request access, rectification, deletion, or portability of your personal data. Contact us at privacy@marxiacafe.example to exercise these rights or to file a complaint. We respond within 30 days.

7. Updates

We periodically update this Privacy Policy to reflect service or regulatory changes. The “Last updated” date below indicates the latest revision.

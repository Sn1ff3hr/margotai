Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Marxia Cafe y Bocaditos (“we”, “us”) uses cookies and similar technologies on the Marxia Cafe y Bocaditos ordering experience.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help websites operate, enhance performance, and deliver personalized experiences. We classify cookies according to their function and impact on privacy.

2. Cookies We Use

  • Strictly necessary: remembers language, theme, and session integrity so the Service remains accessible. These are always enabled when you interact with the ordering workflow.
  • Analytics (optional): aggregated metrics on menu engagement and conversion, activated only after you choose “Accept All” in the cookie banner.
  • Functional (optional): GPS preferences or saved delivery instructions, enabled through “Accept Necessary” or “Accept All”.

3. Managing Consent

The consent banner presents three choices:

  • Accept All enables all cookie categories.
  • Reject All disables optional cookies and clears previously stored analytics data.
  • Accept Necessary keeps only essential cookies required for secure operation.

You can revisit your choice by clearing the “cookie-consent” record from your browser or contacting us for assistance.

4. Third Parties

Trusted processors assisting with analytics or delivery must comply with NIST CSF, CISA Cyber Essentials, and PCI DSS requirements. Any third-party cookies follow equivalent consent controls and are documented in our vendor register.

5. Data Retention

Consent records are stored for up to 12 months unless you withdraw consent earlier. Analytics data is aggregated and retained for trend analysis for up to 24 months.

6. Contact

For questions about this Cookie Policy or to exercise your privacy rights, email privacy@marxiacafe.example.
